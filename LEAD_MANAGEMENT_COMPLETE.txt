## 🔌 API ENDPOINTS USED:

1. **GET** `/api/v1/leads/list` - Get all leads
2. **GET** `/api/v1/leads/list?status=new` - Filter by status
3. **POST** `/api/v1/leads/create` - Create new lead
4. **PUT** `/api/v1/leads/{id}` - Update lead
5. **DELETE** `/api/v1/leads/{id}` - Delete lead
6. **POST** `/api/v1/leads/{id}/convert` - Convert to customer
