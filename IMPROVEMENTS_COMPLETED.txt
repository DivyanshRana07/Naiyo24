  - Fields: name, email, phone, company, status, notes, source
  - Status pipeline: new → contacted → qualified → converted → lost
  - Tracks converted_to_customer_id when converted
  
- 📄 Created: schemas/lead_schema.py
  - LeadCreateRequest, LeadUpdateRequest, LeadResponse
  
- 📄 Created: services/lead_service.py
